About Vision Guard

Vision Guard is an advanced AI-powered eye defect detection system designed to revolutionize eye healthcare. Our platform enables users to scan their eyes, upload datasets for in-depth analysis, detect defects, and receive personalized reports with AI assistance. We are committed to providing accurate, secure, and user-friendly solutions for both patients and healthcare professionals.

  • AI-based eye defect detection
  • Personalized diagnostic reports
  • Secure patient data management
  • Easy-to-use interface for all users
  • Continuous improvement through user feedback
